About Me!

Hi there! My name is Francis Roderique, I am 21 years old, and I am a senior at Michigan State University. I was born on Easter! April 11th, 2004, in Bloomington, IN. On Halloween of that year, I moved to Ann Arbor where I was raised for the rest of my upbringing. I attended Bach and Burns Park elementary, Tappan middle school, and Pioneer (Ann Arbor) high school.

I would consider myself an athlete, although I do not participate in organized sports anymore, growing up I played a variety of sports and found some success in all of them.
Sports I played growing up:

  • soccer
  • baseball
  • swimming
  • basketball
  • ice hockey
  • cross country
  • water polo

I believe athletics are an important part of shaping people, and being involved in a variety of teams allows people to become more adaptable and malleable to do whatever they need to do, and deal with whatever life throws their way. I would consider myself a hard worker, but an introvert and I believe participating in athletics throughout my childhood helped me come out of my shell and was a space for me to be comfortable in doing so.

For the past five or six years I have worked in Ann Arbor at the canoe liveries at Argo and Gallup Park through the city of Ann Arbor's parks and recreation department. I have climbed the ladder within that structure and have been a supervisor for the past three seasons (years). I have written one of my blogs on this topic, as well as a blog about sports on my main page.
